Priyal Suneja

About

I am a Computer Science, Mathematics and Education enthusiast working towards a BS in Computer Science at UC San Diego. I am currently researching under Prof Mia Minnes to increase the functionality of FlapJS, an online tool used to build, simulate, and test automata and other formal models of computation. In the past, I have researched with professors Sorin Lerner and William Griswold on Crowdsourcing Software Verification via Gamification and have interned at ServiceNow as a Software Engineering Intern.

When I'm not coding, I like to work out and listen to indie rock music. I'm also an advocate for diversity in STEM and try to do my part by serving as the Vice President of Women In Computing @ UCSD
